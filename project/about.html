About — 01  ·  Depth across the craft
Yujin Kim Korea × USA SSU · FSU

Yujin Kim is a Korean filmmaker who first fell in love with directing through acting. Determined to learn the craft from every angle before stepping behind the camera, she spent two years in the AD department on Korean film sets, then moved into post-production as a VFX project manager.

She recently completed her MFA in Directing at Florida State University's College of Motion Picture Arts. Having worked in Korea's commercial film industry and trained in the American filmmaking system, Yujin can adapt to nearly any set or environment. Her ultimate goal is to become the Korean Edgar Wright or Guy Ritchie.
